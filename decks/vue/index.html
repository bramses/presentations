Ancient Philososophy

The Creative Process

Preparation - focusing your mind on the problem's dimensions

Incubation - the problem is internalized into the unconscious mind

Intimation - the creative person gets a "feeling" that a solution is on its way

Illumination - the creative idea bursts into conscious awareness
